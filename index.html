The Challenge

Real-World Healthcare Gap

Addressing critical challenges in preventative nutritional care

Data Accuracy Crisis

Clinical dietitians and physicians lack accurate, continuous data on the dietary habits of at-risk individuals between clinic visits. Manual food logging is notoriously error-prone, leading to unreliable patient data.

At-Risk Populations

Pre-diabetic patients and other vulnerable groups require consistent nutritional monitoring. Traditional methods fail to capture real-time eating behaviors, hindering effective preventative care strategies.

Global Health Impact

The inability to monitor dietary habits accurately impedes progress toward global health sustainability targets. Better tools are essential for achieving population-level health improvements.

Our Solution

Hybrid Multimodal AI Technology

Cutting-edge vision and language models working together for precise nutritional analysis

Intelligent Two-Stage Processing

Vision-Language Model Analysis

External Vision-Language Model identifies ingredients and estimates relative portion sizes (small, medium, large) from user-submitted meal photos with high accuracy.

Clinical Reasoning Engine

Flextoken gpt-oss:20b model acts as the clinical reasoning engine, cross-referencing visual inputs to calculate accurate macronutrients (proteins, carbs, fats) for comprehensive dietary assessment.

Continuous Monitoring

Real-time data collection between clinic visits provides healthcare professionals with unprecedented insight into patient dietary patterns, enabling proactive interventions.
