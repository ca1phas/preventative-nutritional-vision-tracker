Understanding Macronutrients

Protein

Essential for building and repairing tissue. It keeps you feeling full for longer, reducing cravings.

Target: 10% - 35% of daily calories

Carbohydrates

Your body's primary energy source. Focus on complex carbs like whole grains to avoid sudden blood sugar spikes.

Target: 45% - 65% of daily calories

Fats

Crucial for brain function and nutrient absorption. Prioritize healthy unsaturated fats (like avocados and nuts).

Target: 20% - 35% of daily calories

Sugar

Excessive added sugar leads to rapid blood glucose spikes, which can cause long-term metabolic and vision issues.

Target: Under 50g per day

Fiber

Slows down digestion and sugar absorption, playing a vital role in preventing diabetes and managing weight.

Target: 25g - 30g per day

Tips for a Balanced Diet

  • Stay Hydrated: Sometimes thirst is confused with hunger. Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Fiber is your friend: High-fiber foods (vegetables, beans, oats) help regulate blood sugar, which aligns with preventative nutritional goals.
  • Mindful Eating: Log your meals before or while you eat to stay aware of your health status throughout the day.
